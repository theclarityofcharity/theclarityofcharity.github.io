Our Mission

Healing is Possible.
Innocence Can Be Restored.

Human trafficking is the second most lucrative crime in the world. Every minute of every day, children are being exploited, sold, and silenced. The Clarity of Charity was founded on the unwavering conviction that these children deserve more than rescue ‚Äî they deserve to truly heal.

Our mission is to create quality holistic aftercare communities where displaced child survivors receive compassionate, long-term care to grow, thrive, and reclaim their lives. Because rescue is only the beginning.

We aspire to expand with healing sanctuaries across North America, and to unite directly with rescue operations on the front lines ‚Äî bringing rescue and restoration together as one seamless, powerful movement.

"Healing yourself is connected with healing others. When we learn how to heal ourselves, we heal our family, our community ‚Äî and the ripple effect positively impacts the world."
‚Äî Jennifer Coburn, Founder
How We Heal

Three Pillars of Survivor Care

Trauma fragments the soul. True healing must address the whole person ‚Äî every dimension of their being.

◈

Mind

Trauma-focused cognitive behavioural therapy, complex trauma-informed care, and professional support for survivors of extreme sexual abuse. Children are given a clear understanding of their pain ‚Äî and shown what healing and health truly look like.

◈

Body

Medical care addressing physical trauma, malnutrition, infection, and the lasting health consequences of repeated abuse. Our communities are staffed 24 hours a day, 7 days a week by trauma-informed specialists whose life mission is to assist in healing with love and compassion.

◈

Spirit

Safe, unconditionally loving communities where children are cherished and nurtured on their empowering healing journey. The body heals with play. The mind heals with laughter. The spirit heals with joy ‚Äî and every child deserves joy.

Our Vision

A Community Built on Belonging

What we are building is more than a facility ‚Äî it is a community. The children who come through our doors will have experienced traumas so profound that even with deep healing, they carry a world within them that few outside can truly understand or relate to.

Rather than reintegrating these children prematurely into environments where they may feel further isolated or misunderstood, our model is built with great intentionality: these children will heal and grow together, surrounded by peers who share similar lived experiences. Within this community they will find something rare and irreplaceable ‚Äî the safety of being truly known, the comfort of not having to explain, and the strength that comes from rising together.

This is not seclusion. This is belonging.
This is the foundation upon which genuine healing is built.
